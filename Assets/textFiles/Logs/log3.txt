sea floor Research Base Log:

Our research team has maintained a cautious coexistence with the enigmatic alien creatures dwelling in these depths. We've closely observed them while keeping a wary distance. However, a group of particularly aggressive specimens launched a sudden, ferocious attack on our facility. They swarmed our base with savage intent, their movements swift and coordinated. The onslaught was gruesome and relentless, leading to horrifying casualties among our team, and the base was plunged into chaos. Our relationship with these creatures has now spiraled into a nightmare, and we're urgently reassessing our approach to ensure the safety of our remaining crew members and the continuation of our mission. Time is running out...
